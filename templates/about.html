Welcome to the TechSwap pre-alpha preview. The idea of TechSwap is to provide a marketplace for people to swap (no cash involved) tech items that they may have lying around but no longer need.
Techswap are just a facilitator to allow you to list items and put you in contact with other people who may have something you would like to swap something for.
Negotiations are purely down to the swaper and swapee, we are just here to provide a platform for you to list your items on.
